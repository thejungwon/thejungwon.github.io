# Researcher Breakfast Talk: A Better Way to Share Data

I gave a **Researcher Breakfast** seminar hosted by **The Department of Research** and the **University Library** at UiS.

## Session

**A better way to share data**

This session introduced how the UiS research centre **NCS2030** is building a federated knowledge cloud to support cross-disciplinary data sharing among energy stakeholders and research organizations. I discussed the significance and practical advantages of cross-domain collaboration from a machine-learning perspective.

## Event Details

- Time: **September 7, 09:00-10:00**
- Place: **University Library, Ullandhaug**
- Audience: **PhD candidates and postdoctoral fellows at UiS**
