# Publication from Master Thesis Supervision (Espen Sele)

Published: **December 22, 2025**

A work related to the Master thesis of **Espen Sele**, co-supervised with **Prof. Ferhat Ozgur Catak**, was published.

## Published Title

**Secure federated learning via neural cryptography with homomorphic operations**

Authors: **Espen Sele**, **Ferhat Ozgur Catak**, **Jungwon Seo**, **Murat Kuzlu**

## Brief Summary

The paper studies neural cryptography with homomorphic operations as a secure aggregation alternative for federated learning. It introduces a neural homomorphic operation system and reports competitive results on MNIST (including 88.10% accuracy with quantized weights), showing its potential as a lightweight option compared with traditional homomorphic encryption schemes.
