# Paper Accepted at IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology

On **April 9, 2026**, our paper
**"Phase-Synchronization-based Federated Learning for Edge Computing Over Decentralized Networks"**
was accepted at **IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology** (**JCR IF 7.1**).

- [Paper](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11482540)

## Summary

In this work, we propose **FedKuramoto**, a novel federated learning algorithm inspired by the phase-synchronization dynamics of the Kuramoto oscillator model.

## My Contribution

I mainly led the **large-scale experimental design and execution** for this study.
