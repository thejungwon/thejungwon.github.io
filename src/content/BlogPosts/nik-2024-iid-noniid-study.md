# NIKT 2024 Publication: Understanding Federated Learning from IID to Non-IID

Published: **November 24, 2024**

**Understanding Federated Learning from IID to Non-IID dataset: An Experimental Study**

Authors: **Jungwon Seo**, **Ferhat Ozgur Catak**, **Chunming Rong**

Conference: **NIKT 2024: Norwegian ICT Conference for Research and Education**  
Host: **University of Bergen**  
Location: **Bergen, Norway**  
Dates: **November 25-27, 2024**

- [Publication Page](https://www.ntnu.no/ojs/index.php/nikt/article/view/6226)

![IID vs Non-IID loss landscape](/images/news/iid-noniid-landscape.png)

## Brief Summary

This study examines why federated learning performance drops under non-IID client data. The paper highlights that inconsistent client loss landscapes are a key cause of degradation, and organizes existing solutions into two broad strategies: adjusting parameter update paths and modifying client loss landscapes.
