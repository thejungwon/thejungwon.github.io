# Ph.D. Thesis Officially Submitted

On **April 8, 2026**, my Ph.D. thesis was officially submitted.

## Title

**Toward Federated AI: Integrating Reliable Training, Automated Deployment, and Secure Inference**

## Summary

This thesis reframes federated learning as part of a broader “Federated AI” paradigm, addressing the full lifecycle of decentralized AI systems—including training, deployment, privacy-preserving inference, and knowledge-level collaboration across autonomous organizations.
