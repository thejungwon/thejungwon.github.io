# ProxyLLM Published in ECML PKDD 2025 Demo Track

Our paper was accepted and published in the **ECML PKDD 2025 Demo Track**.
Published online: **October 2, 2025**.

**ProxyLLM: LLM-Driven Framework for Customer Support Through Text-Style Transfer**

Authors: **Sehyeong Jo**, **Jungwon Seo**

I participated as the **corresponding author** in this collaboration.

- [Publication Page](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-032-06129-4_28)
- [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-032-06129-4_28)

![ProxyLLM architecture](/images/news/proxyllm-architecture.png)

## Brief Summary

The work proposes an LLM-driven framework that transfers customer message style to reduce emotional burden on support agents while preserving actionable content.
