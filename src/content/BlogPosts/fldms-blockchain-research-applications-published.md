# Federated Large Domain Model System Published

Our paper was published in **Blockchain: Research and Applications**, **Volume 6, Issue 3** (September 2025), Article **100277**.
The journal is indexed in **ESCI** with an **Impact Factor of 5.6**.

**Federated Large Domain Model System**

Authors: **Chunming Rong**, **Jungwon Seo**, **Zihan Zhao**, **Ferhat Ozgur Catak**, **Jiahui Geng**, **Martin Gilje Jaatun**

I participated in this publication as the **corresponding author**.

- [Paper](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2096720925000041)
- [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.bcra.2025.100277)

## Brief Summary

The paper proposes the Federated Large Domain Model System (FLDMS), a conceptual framework for collaborative domain-specific foundation model development across private clouds, with a focus on secure cross-organization cooperation and efficient resource utilization.
