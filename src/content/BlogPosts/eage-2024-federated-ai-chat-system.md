# Presented at 85th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition

I presented the following work at the **85th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition**:

**Federated AI Chat System: Tackling Tacit Knowledge Management**

Authors: **Jungwon Seo**, **Boyu Cui**, **Chunming Rong**

Conference details:
- Event: **85th EAGE Annual Conference & Exhibition**
- Dates: **June 10-13, 2024**
- Location: **Oslo, Norway**

- [Paper Page](https://www.earthdoc.org/content/papers/10.3997/2214-4609.202410298)
- [DOI](https://doi.org/10.3997/2214-4609.202410298)

![Federated AI Chat System overview](/images/news/federated-ai-chat-system-overview.png)

## Brief Summary

The work presents a federated AI chat system (GPTWell) for tacit knowledge management in oil and gas, using LLM-based assistants and retrieval-augmented generation while preserving organizational data boundaries.
